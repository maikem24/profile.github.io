Why I chose Freelancing

In todays fast pacing work environment, I think you have to decide early which place you wanna take. So I asked myself - do I wanna be a specialist or a generalist?

For sure it is nice to dig deep in one area and become a real expert in it. To always be able to deliver the right answers because people know you are the best in your field.
But imagining to be able to dive into different industries, develop a rich set of useful skills, get your head into new things and overcome challenges while constantly learning and growing? That idea suited me much more.

So I chose to become a Freelancer. I like the idea of working for more than one company at a time. It gets me out of my comfort zone because it is always a new start. New systems, working structures and communication ways, new colleagues and social structures, new industries and rules. As well as my personal interests change from time to time, my working interests might do as well. I am excited to work myself into new things and passionate about every new project I will have the chance to take on.
